Our history


A garage box in The Hague is the place where the adventure started in 2008, with two friends Hasan Tasdemir and Pascal Rappailles. The garage box soon became too small, after which they moved to a warehouse on an industrial estate. Unfortunately, the shed was not heated and in the middle of winter everything had to be transferred to an empty Ministry of Agriculture. When this building also had to be emptied, the then collection of 10 arcade video games with some spare parts was stored in an old showroom for classic cars. Because there was no room to renovate or play with the cabinets, they looked for suitable space.


It was found in 2011 on the Cobaltstraat in Zoetermeer. There Hasan decided to rent the location with 4 friends and the adventure could grow into its own meeting place to practice their hobby and relax with a game.
